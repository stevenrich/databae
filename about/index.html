the databae woodshop is a small woodshop based out of the living room of journalist steven rich. rich has been building on and off over the past 15 years, opening his own woodshop in 2018 and formalizing the shop into a business in 2020. the shop is based out of southwest washington, dc and is open when rich isn't working his day job and when he feels like it. the shop specializes in the design and constuction of unique wood furniture and objects, built to the specifications of customers and their space and then never rebuilt for others.
